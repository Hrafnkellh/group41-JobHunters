Frequently asked questions

To create an account, click the "Sign Up" button at the top-right corner of the homepage. Enter your email address and choose a strong password. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the registration process

To search for job listings, go to the "Jobs" section and use the search bar at the top. You can filter job listings by title, company, location, and other criteria to find the most relevant opportunities.

To edit your profile, click on your profile picture or name at the top-right corner, and select "Edit Profile" from the dropdown menu. Update your information and save the changes.

To apply for a job, find the job listing you're interested in and click on it to view the details. Then click the "Apply" button and follow the instructions to submit your application.

To track the status of your applications, click on your "Profile" button at the top-right corner of the page. Scroll down to the "Job Applications" section where you can view the status of all your applications. Each job application card displays the job title, status, employer, and options to view or delete the application.
